Who We Are

Shiv Shakti Tours is a dedicated travel service focused on pilgrimage, cultural and family journeys across India and Nepal. Our work is built on experience, discipline and personal responsibility.

We regularly conduct tours to Bodh Gaya, Varanasi, Rajgir, Gorakhpur, Nepal and many other important destinations. Every journey is planned with attention to comfort, safety and spiritual peace.

Our Journey

Founded in Faith

Shiv Shakti Tours began with a simple mission: to provide safe, comfortable, and meaningful pilgrimage experiences. Our founders, having traveled extensively across sacred sites in India and Nepal, understood the challenges pilgrims face and sought to create a service that addresses these needs with compassion.

From humble beginnings conducting small group tours to Bodh Gaya, we have grown into a trusted name in spiritual tourism, serving thousands of devotees from across India and abroad.
